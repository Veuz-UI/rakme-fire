Our Values

Innovation

Workplace safety is a major concern, and employers must ensure that the work environment is safe. In work-related injuries, people tend to blame the organization when a mishappening occurs. Injuries at the workplace can affect both the employees and the employers. An employer's reputation will be damaged, whereas the employees will have to face serious health complications. It is one of the critical reasons why employers must provide safety products to their employees. At Rakme Safety, we deal with a range of safety products that will prepare you and your employees for any health risk.

Safety products that save lives

Be it eyes, hands, or ears, all our body parts are prone to different risks and injuries. In industries or workplaces where there is the use of chemicals, toxic gases, and substances, safety becomes critical. Here are the common safety products that are required at the workplace:

  • Safety goggles for eye protection
  • Safety footwear for footwear protection
  • Eyewash stations
  • Earmuffs and earphones for ear protection
  • Gloves for hand protection
  • Safety jackets and vests
  • Arrester and harness for fall protection

With 16 years of experience, we offer a wide range of safety and workwear protective clothing that will protect against potential hazards.

Integrity

Here are some of the benefits of workwear protective clothing:

  • Protection against injury - One of the biggest benefits of safety equipment is that they protect against injury. Wearing appropriate safety equipment minimizes the risk of injury and will keep them safe. Workers in chemical, manufacturing, construction and other industries are susceptible to different injuries. It is mandatory for them to take necessary precautions and wear the right safety equipment.
  • Less sickness - In critical cases, employees take some time off work. The right workwear protective equipment will ensure that employers are safe and less sick.
  • Comply with the law guidelines - The workers can even take a legal step against an employer, and you will have to provide them the compensatory pay. However, when you are taking proper measures for their safety, they won't be able to take legal action. You can prove that you were taking all the necessary measures and it is not your fault.
  • Reputation - It will not only save you from paying hefty fines. But providing safety equipment will ensure that your organization's reputation is not at risk. Your business will be affected, and there are chances that employees will avoid working at your place because of the bad reputation.
